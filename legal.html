Koinslot INC. Privacy Policy

Overview

At Koinslot Inc, your privacy is paramount. Our core revenue comes from our exceptional offerings in gaming and technology, not through the exploitation of your personal details.

Data Minimization

We aim to minimize data collection. When necessary, we're transparent about the reasons, limit the scope, maintain it only for required durations, and empower you to opt-out.

Data Security and Legal Disclosure

Robust safeguards protect your data. Only essential personnel access your information, and legal imperatives may necessitate disclosure to authorities.

Transactions on Koinslot Inc

Purchasing from our platforms requires specific personal details to fulfill orders, manage licenses, and support services. We avoid third-party sharing unless integral to transaction completion or shipment processing.

Data Handling During Purchases

Credit card details bypass our servers, transiting directly to payment processors, thus enhancing security. Your contact information is manageable anytime via support@kywy.io.

Support Interactions

Support interactions necessitate the collection of contact details and relevant data to resolve your queries effectively.

Analytics and Usage

Anonymized data informs our product improvement processes. Opt-out options are available for analytics and crash reports, aligning with our respect for user choice.

Data Retention

We hold onto data as needed for support and development insights. Requests for data removal can be directed to support@kywy.io.

Updates and Communications

Automatic checks keep your software up-to-date. News about Koinslot Inc products reaches you through our carefully managed communication channels.

Rights and Choices

You have control over your information, with clear paths to update preferences, and rights under privacy laws are fully supported.

Contact and Changes

Reach out to support@kywy.io for privacy inquiries. Our policy will evolve alongside regulatory and operational landscapes.

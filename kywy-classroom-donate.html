Our Mission

We believe every student deserves access to modern technology and programming education. The KYWY platform provides an affordable, engaging way for students to learn programming, game development, and electronics in an interactive, hands-on environment.

How Your Donation Helps

Your contribution directly supports:

  • KYWY devices for disadvantaged classrooms
  • Educational materials and curriculum development
  • Teacher training and ongoing support
  • Programming workshops and after-school programs
  • Student competitions and showcases

Powered by Betabox.com

Our classroom experiences are provided by Betabox.com, ensuring professional, engaging educational content. We focus on open source curriculum development, making our educational materials freely available to educators worldwide.

Open Source Education

All curriculum materials developed through this program will be:

  • Open source and freely available on GitHub
  • Community-driven with educator input and feedback
  • Continuously improved through collaborative development
  • Accessible to schools regardless of budget constraints
